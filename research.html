Research Interests

Satyabrata Mahapatra - Postdoctoral Researcher at Sungkyunkwan University

Beyond Standard Model Physics

My research explores various aspects of physics beyond the Standard Model, aiming to address fundamental questions in particle physics and cosmology.

Figure 1: Illustration of Beyond Standard Model Physics

Neutrino Physics

I investigate neutrino mass mechanisms, mixing patterns, and their implications for particle physics and cosmology.

Figure 2: Neutrino oscillation diagram

Dark Matter Phenomenology

My work includes studying various dark matter models, their detection prospects, and cosmological implications.

Figure 3: Dark matter distribution in the universe

Leptogenesis and Baryogenesis

I explore mechanisms for generating the observed matter-antimatter asymmetry in the universe.

Figure 4: Schematic of leptogenesis process

Primordial Black Holes and Gravitational Waves

My research extends to the study of primordial black holes and their potential as sources of gravitational waves.

Figure 5: Gravitational wave spectrum from primordial black holes

Cosmological Tensions

I work on addressing key cosmological challenges, including the Hubble and S-8 tensions, through innovative theoretical frameworks.

Figure 6: Hubble tension visualization

Particle Physics Anomalies

I investigate explanations for observed anomalies like the muon (g-2), connecting them to broader theoretical frameworks in particle physics.
